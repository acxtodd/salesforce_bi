## Executive Summary

The "Bad Gateway" (502) error reported during smoke testing was caused by an `ImportError` in the Answer Lambda (`cannot import name 'ResponseStream'`). This was due to the `aws-lambda-powertools` library missing the expected streaming utility in the installed version.

We resolved the 502 error by removing the dependency on the `ResponseStream` class. The Lambda now executes successfully but falls back to **buffered response mode** (non-streaming). This restores service availability but results in higher latency (~7-9s) compared to the streaming target.
